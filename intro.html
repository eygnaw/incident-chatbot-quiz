Incident Decision-Making Quiz

This quiz presents short clinical-governance scenarios drawn from the Incident Decision Tree. For each situation you must choose the management option you believe is safest and most proportionate.

How it works

  • All questions appear on a single scrollable page.
  • The timer starts when you press “Begin” and stops when you submit.
  • You cannot change answers after submission, but you will see explanations for every scenario.
  • The IDT chatbot stays visible on the right—use it at any point for guidance.

Your data

Your choices and feedback are recorded anonymously for service-improvement only. No personal identifiers are stored.
